Deep Reefs

Deep Reefs is a website that provides information about coral reefs. This website shares information about coral reefs around the world. It explains why coral reefs are important, the different types of reefs, and the dangers they face, like climate change and pollution. The website was created as part of an Informatics project for SMA Santa Maria 1 Bandung. Its goal is to help people understand and care more about coral reefs, encouraging them to protect these amazing ecosystems.

About Me
I created Deep Reefs to share informations about coral reefs and to raise awareness about their importance and the threats they face. Through this site, I hope to educate and inspire others to appreciate and protect these vital marine ecosystems, and also to hopefully get a good grade.
